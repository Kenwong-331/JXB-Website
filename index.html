What is EMS?

EMS (Electrical Muscle Stimulation) helps activate muscles through controlled impulses while you perform guided movements. It’s popular for time-efficient training — especially for busy people.

Time-efficient

Short sessions built for busy schedules — commonly around 20 minutes.

Coach-guided

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for first-time EMS clients.

Does EMS hurt?

EMS feels different from normal exercise, but the intensity is adjusted based on your comfort and fitness level.

Is EMS suitable for beginners?

Yes. First-timers are welcome, and our coach will guide you step by step during the session.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable workout clothing. If anything specific is needed, we will let you know before your session.

How long is one session?

A typical EMS session is around 20 minutes, making it suitable for busy schedules.
